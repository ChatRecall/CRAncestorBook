# Sensitive Interview Questions

> **Note:** Limit audience for these questions as appropriate.

---

## I. Relationships and Heartbreaks

- Can you tell me about some of the difficulties you've experienced in your relationships?
- Is there a love that you've never forgotten?
- Have you ever experienced heartbreak?
- If you could do it over again, would you choose the same partner(s)? Why or why not?

---

## II. Regrets, Failures, and Lessons Learned

- Looking back on your life, is there anything you wish you had done differently?
- What do you think was the biggest mistake you made in life? How did it affect you?
- What's one thing you wish you could go back and tell your younger self?
- What do you consider your biggest failures?
- What was the hardest lesson for you to learn?
- What was a time when you realized you were wrong about something you previously believed?
- Is there something you've learned about life that you wish everyone knew?

---

## III. Reconciliation and Relationships

- Have you ever had a falling out with someone that you regret? What happened?
- Was there a time when you forgave someone, or they forgave you, and it made a big difference?
- If you drifted apart… why did we drift apart?

---

## IV. Impact on Others and Unexpressed Feelings

- Did you know how much I loved you?
- Did you know how much you influenced me?
- Did I make you proud?
- Is there anything you never told me but wished you had?
- What hopes did you have for me?
- What advice would you give me for my future?

---

## V. Perspectives on Death and Afterlife

- Are you afraid of dying?
- What do you think happens after we die?
- Do you have any messages for those of us left behind?
- If you could write your own epitaph, what would it say?
- What scares you most?
